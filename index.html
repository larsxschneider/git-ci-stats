The bar represents the sum of the run times of all parallel jobs of a build. A gray bar indicates a compile or an unknown error. A red bar indicates a successful build but test case errors.

The last column indicates the failure reason. "no-log" means that TravisCI generated nothing, "stalled" means the build just stopped for an unknown reason, "compile" means that compilation failed, txxxx" means that this test failed in at least one job of the build.

You can click on the Git hash to jump to the commit and you can click on the bar to jump to the TravisCi build.
